UNUM DECENNIUM ET DIMIDIUM AMORIS

2022 VALENTINE'S SPECIAL

I dedicate this year's Valentine's Day to my closest cousins who have been with me since the start. You were all there for me since we were little and treated me as your own sibling. And as an only child, I will always treasure it until the end. We've had adventures that we'll probably never experience again, we tell each other stupid jokes until we cry our eyes out from laughing, we kept secrets that only us will ever know, and so we made memories which made our bond stronger. This is as cheesy as it can get but I geniunly wish for you, my kuyas and ates, the best, and that I love you all (no homo).
